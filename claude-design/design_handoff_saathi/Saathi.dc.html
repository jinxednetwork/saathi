Saathi

A calm, gently gamified companion for students under exam pressure. Build your own saathi, journal your mood, focus in study sprints, and talk things through — with reward loops borrowed from the best habit apps and the anxiety loop left behind.

Design principles

Calm energy, not anxious energy. The reward loop encourages; it never guilt-trips. Missed days get "welcome back", never a funeral.

The mascot is the emotional anchor. Users build their own — it reacts to mood and consistency, including a caring "concerned" state.

Safety is never gamified. The crisis surface renders calm and serious — no XP, no mascot cheer, no confetti.
